Organization Mandate

Acres of Peace convenes and implements projects and programs targeted towards empowering individuals to live a prosperous life and promoting a just and peaceful society. We envision a world where people are willing and able to live in harmony despite their differences. Thus, we strive to empower individuals and communities to build resilience against violence and intolerance towards others.

Adovacy

We aim to influence individuals, organizations, and governments towards decisions that promote a peaceful and just society.

Communication

We understand that inadequate understanding of the position of others can lead to tensions and violence. Hence we promote effective communication across divides to foster interaction, clear misconceptions, and build bridges towards harmonious relationships.

Religious Freedom

We believe that everyone has freedom of religion and belief, and affirm that inter-religious engagement or peaceful co-existence does not require compromising one’s fundamental religious beliefs.

Education

We believe that continuous education is necessary for mutual trust, understanding, and collaboration for a just and peaceful society.

Support

We are motivated to work towards the reduction of structural injustice worldwide and to provide support to underprivileged and vulnerable individuals and communities.

Our Mission

Acres of Peace convenes and implements projects and programs targeted towards empowering individuals to live a prosperous life and promoting a just and peaceful society. We envision a world where people are willing and able to live in harmony despite their differences. Thus, we strive to empower individuals and communities to build resilience against violence and intolerance towards others.

Our Vision

Building a peaceful society, one person, one family, one community at a time!
